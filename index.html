Common Questions

FAQ About Tulum Transportation

How long is the drive from Cancún Airport to Tulum?

The journey typically takes 1 hour 45 minutes to 2 hours depending on traffic conditions along Highway 307. Our drivers know the best routes to keep travel time minimal.

Is it safe to travel by private transfer?

Absolutely. Our Tulum transportation service uses only licensed, vetted drivers and insured vehicles — significantly safer than street taxis.

What if my flight is delayed or cancelled?

We monitor all flights in real time. If your flight is delayed, your driver waits at no extra charge. For cancellations, we offer full flexibility on rebooking.

Can I book a return transfer back to Cancún Airport?

Yes! We offer both one-way and round-trip Tulum transportation. Book your return at the same time and save on the round trip.

Are car seats available for young children?

Yes, child safety seats are available on request at the time of booking at no extra cost. Just mention the ages of your children when reserving.

How do I pay for the transfer?

We accept cash in USD or Mexican Pesos, as well as major credit cards. Payment is made directly to your driver upon arrival or you can prepay online at booking.
